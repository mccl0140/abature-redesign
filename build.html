Handmade Reclaimed and Custom Projects

We offer custom wood working creations that can be personalized to your liking. If you are looking for an irregular item that you can’t find in stores, chances are Dennis can make it. We try to make the process smooth and simple by putting everything out in the open for you. No sneaky catches or fine print.

Firstly, if you have questions you may contact Dennis directly to have them answered. You may also fill out the “Project Request Form" below and send it to abature@hotmail.com if you would like to get a wood working project quote.

Then, Dennis will reply to you with a quote and/or further questions and details if required. We draw up a contract in simple and plain language to deter any confusion. Once a deposit has been paid and the contract outlining all details has been signed, Dennis will be off to work!
